Privacy Policy — Marketing Autopilot

Last updated: June 2026

1. What data we collect

Marketing Autopilot accesses the following data through the Instagram Graph API and Facebook Marketing API, solely with the explicit authorization of the user:

  • Instagram account profile data (username, follower count, media count)
  • Public post data (captions, likes, comments, publication date, media type)
  • Hashtag reach and trend data
  • Google Search Console performance data (search queries, impressions, clicks)

2. How we use the data

Collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Analyze competitor activity on social media platforms
  • Generate content recommendations and posting schedules for the authorized user
  • Display analytics dashboards to the authenticated account owner

We do not sell, share, or transfer data to third parties for advertising or any other purposes.

3. Data storage and retention

Data is stored on servers controlled by the application owner. Instagram and Facebook data is retained for a maximum of 90 days from the date of collection. Users may request immediate deletion at any time.

4. Data deletion

To request deletion of all data associated with your account, send an email to: privacy@marketing-autopilot.dev with subject line "Data Deletion Request". All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

You may also revoke application access at any time via: Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites.

5. Contact

For privacy-related questions: privacy@marketing-autopilot.dev
